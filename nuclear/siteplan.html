Site Purpose: Track nuclear power plant info/outages in the USA

This site provides information regarding different nuclear power plants in the United States. It can provide outage information over time and graph it(?) (I will see if I can figure out how to do this.)

Website Scenarios:

· What is the capacity of different nuclear power plants?

· How has the power output of a certain reactor fluctuated over time?

· What is the current percentage output of different nuclear power plants?

Color Palette:
