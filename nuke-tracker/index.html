A Little About Nuke Tracker

Nuke Tracker is a data-driven web platform designed to monitor, analyze, and visualize nuclear power plant outages across the country. By collecting real-time and historical data on scheduled and unscheduled outages, the site generates dynamic, interactive graphs and dashboards to help users spot trends, assess regional impacts, and better understand the nuclear grid’s operational status.
